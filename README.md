🐾 PetBreedAI
Your Pocket AI for Understanding Pets

PetBreedAI is an intelligent Android application that transforms your smartphone into a powerful pet breed recognition system. With just a photo, users can instantly identify cat and dog breeds, explore predictions, and gain meaningful insights — all powered by on-device AI.

✨ Why PetBreedAI?

Ever wondered:

“What breed is this dog?”
“Is my cat a Bengal or something else?”

PetBreedAI answers these questions in seconds — no internet required.

🎯 Core Experience

📸 Snap or Upload → Analyze → Discover

Capture or select an image
Let the AI analyze it
Instantly view:
Breed prediction
Confidence level
Alternative matches
Helpful breed tips
🧠 AI at the Core

At the heart of PetBreedAI lies a powerful deep learning model:

Architecture: MobileNet (Pre-trained)
Dataset: Oxford-IIIT Pet Dataset
Classes: 37 Cat & Dog Breeds
Deployment: TensorFlow Lite (on-device inference)

⚡ Fast, lightweight, and fully offline
